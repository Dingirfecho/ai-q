§ Solution · Human Resources

HR Automation/

"No paperwork. No lost spreadsheets. No chasing emails."

Your HR team spends hours every week on tasks that add no real value. We automate the full employee lifecycle — from onboarding to offboarding, with everything in between — so your team can focus on people, not administration.

§01 · The problem

Hours lost every week

Building employee file folders, forwarding forms, manually logging leave requests, remembering to send birthday greetings. None of these tasks require HR expertise — yet they consume professional time every single day.

One integrated system, 1 unified flow.

Onboarding, leave, approvals, monthly close, communications — fully coordinated without manual intervention.

§02 · The solution

What the system does

We automate the complete employee lifecycle, with intelligence built into every stage.

01
Employee files in Drive
When a new employee joins, the system automatically creates their folder in Google Drive with the correct structure: personal documents, onboarding, updates, legal, and offboarding. Named per your internal convention.
02
Automated onboarding
You enter new hire data in a spreadsheet. The system creates directories, logs the hire in your updates file, updates the roster, and emails the employee all required forms — automatically.
03
Unplanned leave
The employee notifies via Gmail or Telegram. Gemini reads the message, identifies the employee, extracts the relevant information, and logs the leave — notifying HR and the area manager. Zero manual handling.
04
Planned leave with approval
The employee submits a request by email. AI extracts the data, validates roster membership, and routes the request to the approver. One click to approve — the system logs the event, generates the official document, and sends it.
05
Salary advances
Same approval circuit for advance requests: email submission, limit validation, manager routing. Once approved, everything is recorded and documented automatically.
06
Monthly close
At month end, the system runs itself. It consolidates all events — hires, terminations, leave, advances, salary changes — saves the monthly archive in Drive, and resets the updates file for the next period.
07
Hours bank
Employees log overtime or time-off requests via Telegram with a free-form message. AI interprets, updates the balance, and enforces legal limits. Full compliance with Argentine Labour Modernisation Act (Law 27.802, Art. 197 bis).

§05 · Law 27.802

Hours bank — compliant from day one

Argentina's Labour Modernisation Act (enacted February 2026) formally introduces the hours bank as a legal instrument — and mandates a reliable control method. Our solution meets all three Art. 197 bis requirements out of the box.

▲
Registration via Telegram
The employee sends a free-form message: "Left at 8pm today, that's 2 hours overtime." AI interprets it, identifies the employee by their alias, classifies the movement, and updates the balance. No new app. No portal.
◆
Auto-generated agreement
When HR activates the hours bank for an employee, the system generates the written agreement from a Google Docs template, emails it, and logs acceptance. Legal documentation handled automatically.
◇
Legal limit enforcement
Every registration is checked against the legal weekly maximum and the agreed cap. If a limit is reached, HR receives an automatic alert before the transaction is processed.

§06 · Competitive context

What SAP HCM doesn't solve in Argentina

SAP HCM is the enterprise standard. It's also expensive, rigid, and slow to adapt to Argentine labour reality. This solution was built specifically for what SAP doesn't handle well.

⚠
Real communication channels
SAP lives in self-service portals and corporate email. In Argentina, most leave notifications come via Telegram or WhatsApp. Our solution receives and processes those messages with AI — employees notify naturally, without learning any portal or installing any app.
◆
Collective agreements (CCT)
Argentina has over 1,500 active collective agreements, each with its own rules for special leave, overtime rates, bonuses, and pay scales. Parametrising one in SAP requires certified consultants and weeks of work. In our solution, each CCT's rules are modelled in scenario logic and AI prompts — adaptable in hours when the agreement changes.
◇
Hours bank — Law 27.802
SAP can parametrise it — but only through the Time Management module, at separate implementation cost, requiring complex integration with the payroll module. Our solution includes it from the start: Telegram registration, balance calculation, legal limit enforcement, and automatic Art. 197 bis agreement generation.
▲
4th-category tax withholding (SIRADIG)
The monthly income tax calculation for salaried employees includes deductions for dependants, rent, mortgage interest, and private health insurance. Every AFIP table update in SAP requires a consultant to re-parametrise. Our solution uses editable Google Sheets tables that the HR team can update themselves in minutes — no external dependency.
◆
Family allowances — SUAF
Declaring children, updating marital status, and registering pregnancy with ANSES in SAP is either manual or requires costly add-on modules. Our solution automates document collection, data validation, and generation of required ANSES forms — all archived in the employee's digital file.
◇
Document expiry alerts
Workers' comp insurance, mandatory life insurance, health cards, driver's licences for logistics staff, union certifications. In SAP, this requires costly configuration and ongoing maintenance. With our solution it's one Make scenario, configurable in a day: automatic alerts to HR and the area manager with the right lead time.
▲
Access control cross-referenced with roster
Argentine access control systems (ZKTeco, Anviz, Hikvision) export CSV or have APIs. Our solution automatically cross-references real attendance data against the roster and declared leave — surfacing inconsistencies that SAP can't detect without costly middleware.
◆
AI-powered absenteeism pattern analysis
SAP reports what happened. Our solution anticipates what's about to happen. Gemini automatically detects: recurring absences on specific weekdays, leave clustering by area or period, correlation between leave type and department. HR receives alerts before the pattern becomes a problem.
