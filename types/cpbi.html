Your Path Forward

90-Day Roadmap: Maintain & Extend

Your roadmap isn't about fixing problems‚Äîit's about extending your lead. Focus on talent, disruption awareness, and sharing your success to attract even more talent.

1

Talent Fortification

Days 1-30

Your AI capability is only as strong as your team. Lock in your key people and build bench strength.

Key Tasks

Week 1: Identify your top 10 AI contributors. Schedule 1:1s to understand their career goals and retention risks.
Week 2: Competitive comp analysis. Ensure key people are at 75th+ percentile. Adjust if needed.
Week 3: Create individual development plans for high-potentials. Include conference budget, learning time, stretch projects.
Week 4: Launch internal AI mentorship program. Pair senior practitioners with emerging talent.
‚úì Milestone
Retention plan in place for key talent. Development paths documented. Zero unplanned departures.
2

Disruption Scanning

Days 31-60

Build systematic capability to spot emerging threats and opportunities before competitors.

Key Tasks

Week 5: Assign "disruption scout" role. This person monitors AI research, startups, and competitor moves full-time.
Week 6: Create first Disruption Radar. Map 10 emerging technologies by impact vs. time to maturity.
Week 7: Establish quarterly "What Could Kill Us?" session with leadership. Review radar, prioritize investigations.
Week 8: Launch 1-2 small POCs on highest-potential emerging technologies identified in radar.
‚úì Milestone
Disruption Radar published. Quarterly review cadence established. First exploratory POCs underway.
3

Thought Leadership & Brand

Days 61-90

Share your success externally to attract talent, build brand, and cement your position as an AI leader.

Key Tasks

Week 9: Document 3 AI success stories with measurable outcomes. Get stakeholder approval to share externally.
Week 10: Submit to speak at 2-3 industry conferences. Pitch your unique AI journey and lessons learned.
Week 11: Launch "AI at [Company]" blog or LinkedIn series. Showcase team, culture, and innovation.
Week 12: Host an AI meetup or roundtable. Position your company as a hub for AI practitioners.

Public-facing AI brand established. Conference talks submitted. Inbound talent inquiries increasing.
