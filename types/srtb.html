Your Path Forward

90-Day Roadmap: Wishes to Wins

Your goal is to convert executive enthusiasm into proven results. Start small, measure everything, and build the evidence base for larger investments.

1

Opportunity Mapping

Days 1-30

Capture all the AI wishes floating around and turn them into a prioritized, realistic backlog.

Key Tasks

Week 1: Interview 10 stakeholders. Ask: "Where do you wish AI could help?" Capture everything.
Week 2: Score each opportunity on impact (1-10), feasibility (1-10), and data readiness (1-10).
Week 3: Reality-check top 5 with technical assessment. What would it actually take?
Week 4: Present prioritized backlog to leadership. Get alignment on top 2-3 to pursue.

AI Opportunity Backlog with 15+ scored use cases. Leadership aligned on top priorities.
2

Quick Win Execution

Days 31-60

Deliver 2-3 visible, measurable AI wins that prove the concept and build credibility.

Key Tasks

Week 5: Select 2-3 "quick wins" â€” high impact, low complexity, fast to implement.
Week 6: Define success metrics BEFORE launching. What does "working" look like?
Week 7: Implement quick wins. Use off-the-shelf tools where possible.
Week 8: Measure results. Document wins with specific numbers and stakeholder quotes.

2-3 documented quick wins with measurable ROI. Internal case studies ready to share.

Strategic Pilot Launch

Days 61-90
