Your Path Forward

90-Day Roadmap: Architecture to Adoption

Your goal is to prove value before your perfect platform is ready. Ship something ugly that works. Then iterate.

1

Business Problem Discovery

Days 1-30

Get out of the tech bubble and find out what problems actually need solving urgently.

Key Tasks

Week 1: Interview 5 business leaders. Ask: "What's costing you the most time or money right now?"
Week 2: Shadow frontline workers in 2 departments. See their actual pain points firsthand.
Week 3: Map business problems to your existing technical capabilities. What can you actually solve NOW?
Week 4: Select ONE problem with urgent business need + technical feasibility. Get business sponsor sign-off.
‚úì Milestone
Business Problem Registry created. One problem selected with committed business partner.
2

Ugly MVP Delivery

Days 31-60

Ship something that works in 4 weeks. It doesn't need to be pretty or scalable. It needs to prove value.

Key Tasks

Week 5: Define MVP scope ruthlessly. What's the smallest thing that solves the problem?
Week 6: Build MVP using shortcuts and off-the-shelf components. Resist urge to build "right."
Week 7: Deploy MVP to business partner. Get real users on it immediately.
Week 8: Collect feedback daily. Iterate rapidly. Measure impact on the business problem.
‚úì Milestone
Working MVP in production with real users. Measurable impact on business problem documented.
3

Scale What Works

Days 61-90

Now that you've proven value, you can earn the right to build more of your grand vision.

Key Tasks

Week 9: Document MVP success with hard numbers. Build internal case study.
Week 10: Identify next business problem using same model. Repeat the partner approach.
Week 11: Create Value Delivery Timeline showing monthly business impact, not technical milestones.
Week 12: Present to leadership: "Here's what we delivered, here's what we can do next." Secure expanded mandate.
‚úì Milestone
Proven delivery model. Business credibility established. Organization moving from SPTE toward SPTI (Rogue Lab with Integration).
