Your Path Forward

90-Day Roadmap: Chaos to Control

You can't optimize what you can't see. This roadmap focuses on getting visibility first, then making strategic decisions based on facts, not guesses.

1

Discovery & Inventory

Days 1-30

Find every AI tool in the organization and understand who's using what, why, and at what cost.

Key Tasks

Week 1: Send org-wide survey asking "What AI tools do you use?" Make it anonymous to surface shadow IT.
Week 2: Pull expense reports and credit card statements for AI-related subscriptions.
Week 3: Interview department heads about AI tools their teams use and their wish lists.
Week 4: Compile inventory with owner, cost, use case, and data exposure for each tool.
‚úì Milestone
Complete AI Tool Inventory with 90%+ coverage and total spend calculated.
2

Risk Assessment & Quick Wins

Days 31-60

Identify and mitigate immediate risks while capturing easy consolidation opportunities.

Key Tasks

Week 5: Security review of top 10 tools by data sensitivity. Flag any sending customer/employee data externally.
Week 6: Identify duplicate tools (e.g., 3 different transcription services) and pick one to standardize on.
Week 7: Shut down or consolidate 2-3 redundant tools. Migrate users with support.
Week 8: Implement AI Intake Process for new tool requests. Communicate to all employees.
‚úì Milestone
Risk matrix complete, 2+ tools consolidated, intake process live. First cost savings captured.
3

Foundation & Governance

Days 61-90

Establish ongoing governance and lay groundwork for strategic AI investments.

Key Tasks

Week 9: Define approved tool list and communicate "these are our supported AI tools."
Week 10: Create basic AI usage guidelines (what data can/can't go into AI tools).
Week 11: Establish monthly review cadence for AI inventory and spend.
Week 12: Identify top 3 use cases for strategic investment. Build business cases for Phase 2.
‚úì Milestone
Governance framework in place. Organization has moved from SRTE (Chaos Pilot) toward CRTE (Controlled Experiment).
