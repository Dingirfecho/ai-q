The problem

A spreadsheet that doesn't work for you

If you run your gym on a spreadsheet — like most owners — 80% of the work is already done. What's missing is for that file to stop sitting there and start telling you who hasn't paid, how much each plan brings in, and sending the follow-up emails without you writing them one by one.

"You don't know exactly what each plan earns, or how many late payers you have this month." And every member who drops out without follow-up is revenue walking out the door for no reason.
The solution

Three automations, one spreadsheet

Three independent scenarios that share your existing Google Sheets. Each one solves a concrete pain and runs manually with one click, or on a schedule (every Monday at 9am, for example).

1
Plans Report
Per-plan detail: price, active members and projected revenue. In seconds you know which plan is most profitable and what you should be billing this month.
2
Payment Status
Cross-checks members vs. payments and returns up-to-date list, late payers and amounts owed. Lands in your inbox without you opening the sheet or filtering anything.
3
Reactivation
Identifies dropouts and paused members and sends them a reactivation email in the tone you define. The automation with the highest ROI.

How it works

The architecture, at a glance

This is not a migration to a new system, nor an expensive gym-management tool. It's an intelligence layer on top of your current spreadsheet: the database doesn't change, the people using it don't either. The only thing that changes is that the spreadsheet starts producing reports and communicating on its own.

Three independent scenarios in Make.com read your Google Sheets, process the data and trigger the actions. No servers to maintain, no code to keep alive, no lock-in: if tomorrow you want to tweak an email or the report schedule, everything is visible and editable.
